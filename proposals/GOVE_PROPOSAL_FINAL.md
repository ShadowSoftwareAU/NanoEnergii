## Executive Summary

We propose a strategic partnership with the Gove Indigenous Community to deploy our Nano Energy Eve units, providing clean, reliable, zero-fuel electricity through a fully decentralised model tailored to remote and off-grid needs.

With Rio Tinto scaling back refining operations and major services withdrawing from the region, Gove faces a critical turning point. This partnership offers a path to energy sovereignty, new economic returns, and long-term sustainability, replacing dependency with self-determination.

## The Nano Energy Eve Technology

Eve units are compact, scalable, nano-scale energy solutions designed for remote and challenging environments:

- **Capital Cost:** Approximately USD 2,500 per kW
- **Operating Costs:** Zero fuel consumption; approximately 5% of capital cost annually for maintenance
- **Decentralised Design:** Units install at or near homes, community facilities, or commercial sites
- **Resilience:** Eliminates reliance on expensive diesel generators or long transmission lines; high resilience against extreme weather
- **Custom Regional Models:** Each deployment receives a purpose-built configuration (the "Eve Gove Model") tailored to local climate, load profiles, and community needs

## Why Now: The Gove Opportunity

The Gove Peninsula is at an inflection point:

- Rio Tinto is ceasing alumina refining operations
- Major retailers (Woolworths) are withdrawing
- Essential services (medical, veterinary) are under pressure
- The community needs new economic anchors that don't depend on mining patronage

The Eve Gove Model offers a replacement economic engine: one owned and operated by the community, generating both energy and revenue, with flow-on benefits to every household and business on the peninsula.

## Proposed Deployment Approach

1. **Consultation and Assessment:** On-ground assessment of Gove conditions (climate, load, infrastructure)
2. **Custom Prototyping:** Build and validate the Eve Gove Model for local conditions
3. **Pilot Deployment:** Initial units at key community facilities
4. **Scaled Rollout:** Full community deployment with local workforce
5. **Ongoing Operations:** Locally staffed maintenance, billing and support
